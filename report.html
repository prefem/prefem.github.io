Report Sexual and Gender-Based Violence (SGBV)

If you or someone you know has experienced violence like rape, domestic abuse, sexual harassment, or any other form of harm because of their gender, you can report it here. We are here to help you take action and get the support you need. Reporting is safe, confidential, and can stop the violence.

Femicide

Femicide is when a woman or girl is killed because of her gender.

Attempted Femicide

Attempted femicide is a case where someone tries to kill a woman or girl but failed.

Rape

Rape is when someone forces you to have sex without your consent.

Sexual Harassment

Sexual harassment is when someone touches you, talks to you, or behaves in a sexual way without your consent.

Domestic Violence

Domestic violence is abuse that happens in the home, like beating, insulting, or controlling a partner or family member.
