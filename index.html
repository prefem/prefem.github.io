Welcome to PreFem

Women in Nigeria are facing escalating violence. Every day, more women are attacked, abused, and murdered, often by those they trust. Homes, streets, workplaces, and even public transport have become crime scenes. Nowhere is truly safe.

Every time a crime goes unreported, every time an abuser walks free, every time we look away, another woman is lost, her name forgotten, and her killer unpunished. Silence fuels impunity. Impunity breeds more violence. Only awareness, justice, and action can stop it.
